Groove Guide

Vinyl Pressing Advisor

Not all pressings are created equal.

Search an artist to see their studio albums and find out how many discs a quality pressing should have.

For 33‑1/3 RPM records, each side should carry no more than about 22 minutes of music — beyond that, grooves get cut shallower, and dynamic range begins to suffer.

Note Groove Guide currently accounts for 33‑1/3 RPM pressings only and does not yet factor in 45 RPM album pressings.
